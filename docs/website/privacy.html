Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 31, 2025

Introduction

Japan Travel Dictionary ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Data We Collect

1. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Learning Progress: Phrases you've studied and marked as learned
  • Favorites: Phrases you've bookmarked
  • Custom Phrases: Your personal phrases and notes
  • Travel Journal: Your journal entries and photos

Important: All this data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

2. Information Collected by Third Parties

Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA)
  • IP address
  • App usage data
  • Ad interaction data

Purpose: To show relevant advertisements and measure ad performance.

For more information:

  • Google's Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Partners

Apple In-App Purchase

When you make a purchase to remove ads, transaction data is processed by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

3. Photo Library Access

When you add photos to your travel journal:

  • You grant permission to access your photo library
  • Photos are saved locally on your device only
  • We do not upload or transmit your photos anywhere

How We Use Your Data

  • Learning Progress: To track your study history and show personalized recommendations
  • Favorites & Custom Phrases: To provide quick access to your saved content
  • Travel Journal: To help you document your travel experiences
  • Advertisements: To support the free version of the app

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Google AdMob: For advertising purposes (as described above)
  • Apple: For processing in-app purchases

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: All data is stored locally on your device
  • Right to Erasure: Delete the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Right to Rectification: Edit your data within the app
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Purchase premium to remove ads
  • Right to Object: Opt out of personalized ads in iOS Settings

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

  • All local data is stored using iOS secure storage (SQLite)
  • Photos are stored in the app's private sandbox
  • No data is transmitted over networks (except ad requests)

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). The app is designed for general travel use and does not target children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through app update notifications.
