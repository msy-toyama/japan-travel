Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I use the app offline?
A: All phrases are automatically downloaded when you install the app. You can access everything without an internet connection!
Q: How do I hear the pronunciation?
A: Tap on any phrase card to see the detail view, then tap the speaker icon to hear the native pronunciation.
Q: Can I add my own phrases?
A: Yes! Go to the Home screen and tap "My Phrases" to create and organize your custom phrases.
Q: How do flashcards work?
A: Access flashcards from the Home screen. Study by category, mark phrases as learned, and track your progress. The app will suggest what to study next!
Q: How do I remove ads?
A: Tap the Settings icon, then select "Remove Ads" for a one-time purchase of $4.99. This removes all advertisements permanently.
Q: Can I use this app in Japan without roaming charges?
A: Absolutely! The app works completely offline once downloaded. Perfect for avoiding expensive roaming fees.
Q: How do I use the travel journal?
A: From the Journal tab, tap the + button to create a new entry. Select a phrase you used, add a photo, location, and notes to document your experience.
Q: Is my data backed up?
A: All data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, your custom phrases and journal entries will be lost. We recommend exporting your journal regularly.
